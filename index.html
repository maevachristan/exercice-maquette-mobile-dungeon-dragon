About the Series

Season 3 of Stranger Things-the Netflix show brought to you by the Duffer Brothers-released on July 4th. We won't spoil exactly what dangers the kids (and frankly, the entirety of Hawkins, Indiana) faced this year, although suffice to say that a mind flayer (of sorts) rears its head. And of course, you can also expect a fair amount of Dungeons & Dragons play... or at least attempted, with everything else going on!

If you've followed the show, you know how D&D plays a major role in the lives of these characters. If you played the game yourself back in the 1980's, or inerested to see what's underway with the game today, read on!
